### Drink: Protein Smoothie

#### Ingredients:
- 25g Isopure Protein Powder
- 100g 2% Milk
- 50g Nonfat Greek Yogurt

#### Instructions:
1. In a blender, combine the protein powder, milk, and Greek yogurt.
2. Blend until smooth. For added texture, you can throw in a handful of ice cubes.
3. Pour into a glass and enjoy!

### Breakfast: Oatmeal Bowl

#### Ingredients:
- 99.6g Oats
- 100g 2% Milk
- 50g Nonfat Greek Yogurt
- 25g Almonds (chopped)
  
#### Instructions:
1. Cook the oats according to package instructions using the milk.
2. Once cooked, top with Greek yogurt and sprinkle with chopped almonds.
3. Sweeten with honey or maple syrup if desired, and serve warm.

### Lunch: Chickpea & Tofu Salad

#### Ingredients:
- 200g Firm Tofu (cubed and cooked)
- 150g Chickpeas (cooked)
- 50g Nonfat Greek Yogurt (as a dressing)
- Seasoning (salt, pepper, lemon juice) to taste

#### Instructions:
1. In a large bowl, combine the cooked tofu and chickpeas.
2. Mix in the Greek yogurt and season with salt, pepper, and lemon juice to taste.
3. Serve chilled.

### Afternoon Snack: Tofu and Nut Bowl

#### Ingredients:
- 100g Firm Tofu (cubed and sautéed)
- 25g Almonds 
- 25g Pistachios 

#### Instructions:
1. Sauté the tofu cubes in a non-stick pan until golden brown.
2. In a bowl, combine the sautéed tofu with almonds and pistachios.
3. Enjoy as a protein-packed snack.

### Dinner: Millet and Chickpea Stir-Fry

#### Ingredients:
- 200g Millet (cooked)
- 150g Chickpeas (cooked)
- 100g Firm Tofu (cubed and sautéed)
- Seasonings (garlic, soy sauce, veggies of choice)

#### Instructions:
1. Cook the millet according to package instructions.
2. Sauté the tofu cubes until golden brown and set aside.
3. In the same pan, add garlic and your choice of vegetables, stir-fry until tender.
4. Add in the chickpeas and cooked millet, and stir to combine.
5. Add the sautéed tofu back into the mixture and season with soy sauce. Serve warm.
