### Drink: Protein Shake
**Ingredients:**
- 50g Isopure Protein Powder
- 100g 2% Milk

**Instructions:**
1. In a blender, combine 50g of Isopure Protein Powder with 100g of 2% milk.
2. Blend until smooth. 
3. Optionally, add ice cubes and blend again for a chilled drink.
4. Serve immediately.

### Breakfast: Greek Yogurt Bowl
**Ingredients:**
- 150g Nonfat Greek Yogurt
- 49.6g Oats
- 50g Almonds
- 50g Pistachios

**Instructions:**
1. In a bowl, add 150g of nonfat Greek yogurt.
2. Stir in 49.6g of oats until well mixed.
3. Top with 50g almonds and 50g pistachios for added crunch.
4. Enjoy with a drizzle of honey or fruit if desired (not included in ingredient weights).

### Lunch: Chickpea Tofu Salad
**Ingredients:**
- 200g Firm Tofu
- 150g Chickpea
- Seasonings (salt, pepper, and olive oil - not included in weight)

**Instructions:**
1. Drain and rinse 150g of chickpeas. Set aside.
2. Press and cube 200g of firm tofu.
3. Heat a nonstick pan over medium heat and add the tofu cubes. Cook until golden brown on all sides (around 6-8 minutes).
4. In a large bowl, combine the golden tofu and chickpeas.
5. Season with salt, pepper, and olive oil to taste.
6. Serve warm or chilled.

### Afternoon Snack: Nut Mix
**Ingredients:**
- 50g Almonds
- 50g Pistachios

**Instructions:**
1. Combine 50g of almonds and 50g of pistachios in a bowl.
2. Mix well and enjoy for a nutritious snack.

### Dinner: Millet Bowl
**Ingredients:**
- 200g Millet
- 150g Chickpeas (already used earlier, adjust weight as necessary)
- Additional vegetables (carrots, broccoli — not included in the ingredient weight)

**Instructions:**
1. Rinse 200g of millet under cold water.
2. In a pot, combine the millet and water (ratio 1:2) and bring to a boil.
3. Reduce heat and simmer for about 20 minutes or until the water is absorbed and millet is tender.
4. In a separate pan, heat the chickpeas and any additional vegetables until warmed through.
5. Serve the millet topped with the chickpea and vegetable mix.
