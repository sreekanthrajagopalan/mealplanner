### Drink: Protein Smoothie
**Ingredients:**
- 50g Isopure Protein Powder
- 100g 2% Milk
- 50g Nonfat Greek Yogurt
- 50g Almonds (finely ground)
  
**Instructions:**
1. In a blender, combine the Isopure Protein Powder, 2% milk, Nonfat Greek Yogurt, and ground almonds.
2. Blend until smooth.
3. Serve chilled.
