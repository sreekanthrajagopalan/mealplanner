### Drink: Protein Milkshake
**Ingredients:**
- 50 g Isopure Protein Powder
- 100 g 2% Milk
- 50 g Almonds

**Instructions:**
1. Blend the Isopure Protein Powder and 2% Milk until smooth.
2. Add in the almonds and blend again until well incorporated.
3. Pour into a glass and enjoy.

**Total Weight: 200 g**

---

### Breakfast: Greek Yogurt Parfait
**Ingredients:**
- 150 g Nonfat Greek Yogurt
- 100 g Oats
- 50 g Pistachios

**Instructions:**
1. In a bowl, layer the Nonfat Greek Yogurt at the bottom.
2. Add a layer of oats followed by a layer of pistachios.
3. Repeat the layers until ingredients are used up.
4. Serve immediately.

**Total Weight: 300 g**

---

### Lunch: Tofu Bhurji with Rajma and Millet
**Ingredients:**
- 151.1 g Firm Tofu, crumbled
- 148.1 g Rajma (kidney beans), cooked
- 193.4 g Millet (cooked)

**Instructions:**
1. Heat a pan and sauté the crumbled tofu until slightly crispy.
2. Add the cooked Rajma to the pan and mix well.
3. Serve the tofu and rajma mix over a bed of cooked millet.

**Total Weight: 492.6 g**

---

### Afternoon Snack: Oats and Yogurt Bowl
**Ingredients:**
- 100 g Oats
- 150 g Nonfat Greek Yogurt
- 20 g Walnuts

**Instructions:**
1. In a bowl, combine the oats with Nonfat Greek Yogurt.
2. Top with walnuts for added crunch and flavor.
3. Mix well before consuming.

**Total Weight: 270 g**

---

### Dinner: Rajma & Millet Salad with Nuts
**Ingredients:**
- 148.1 g Rajma (cooked)
- 193.4 g Millet (cooked)
- 50 g Almonds
- 50 g Pistachios
- 20 g Walnuts

**Instructions:**
1. In a large bowl, combine the cooked Rajma and cooked millet.
2. Chop the almonds, pistachios, and walnuts and add to the mixture.
3. Toss everything well and serve as a healthy, hearty salad.

**Total Weight: 462.5 g**
