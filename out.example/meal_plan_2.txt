### 1. Drink: Protein Smoothie
**Ingredients:**
- 50g Isopure Protein Powder
- 100g 2% Milk
- 100g Nonfat Greek Yogurt
- 50g Almonds (ground)

**Instructions:**
1. In a blender, combine Isopure Protein Powder, 2% Milk, and Nonfat Greek Yogurt.
2. Blend until smooth.
3. Add ground almonds for texture and additional flavor.
4. Blend briefly to combine.
5. Serve chilled.
