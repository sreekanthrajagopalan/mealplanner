### Drink: Protein Smoothie
**Ingredients:**
- 30g Isopure Protein Powder
- 50g 2% Milk
- 50g Nonfat Greek Yogurt
- 25g Silken Tofu
- 15g Almonds (blended)

**Instructions:**
1. In a blender, combine the Isopure Protein Powder, 2% milk, Greek yogurt, silken tofu, and blended almonds.
2. Blend until smooth. 
3. Pour into a glass and enjoy your nutritious protein smoothie!

### Breakfast: Tofu Scramble
**Ingredients:**
- 100g Firm Tofu
- 50g Silken Tofu
- 50g Wheat (cooked, as a side or mixed in)

**Instructions:**
1. Crumble the firm tofu into a skillet and cook over medium heat.
2. Add the silken tofu and stir until well combined.
3. Season with salt, pepper, and any spices you prefer.
4. Serve with a side of cooked wheat.

### Lunch: Rajma and Tofu Bowl
**Ingredients:**
- 146.7g Rajma (cooked)
- 100g Firm Tofu (cubed and sautéed)
- 50g Nonfat Greek Yogurt (as a topping)

**Instructions:**
1. Cook the rajma until soft. Drain and mix with salt, cumin, and other spices to taste.
2. Sauté the cubed firm tofu until golden.
3. In a bowl, layer the rajma and sautéed tofu. Top with a dollop of Greek yogurt.

### Afternoon Snack: Almond and Fig Energy Bites
**Ingredients:**
- 20g Fig (dried, chopped)
- 50g Almonds (chopped)
- 25g Nonfat Greek Yogurt (to bind)

**Instructions:**
1. In a bowl, mix the chopped figs, chopped almonds, and Greek yogurt until well combined.
2. Form small balls with the mixture.
3. Chill in the refrigerator for 30 minutes before serving.

### Dinner: Tofu and Wheat Stir-Fry
**Ingredients:**
- 100g Firm Tofu (cubed)
- 150g Silken Tofu (blended)
- 150g Wheat (cooked)
- 15g Pecans (chopped, to add crunch)

**Instructions:**
1. Cubes of firm tofu should be marinated and sautéed until golden.
2. Mix cooked wheat with sautéed tofu.
3. Stir in blended silken tofu to create a creamy sauce.
4. Top with chopped pecans before serving.
