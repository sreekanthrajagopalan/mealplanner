### Drink: Protein Smoothie
**Ingredients:**
- 50.0 gm Isopure Protein Powder
- 100.0 gm 2% Milk
- 50.0 gm Nonfat Greek Yogurt
- 20.0 gm Sunflower Seeds

**Instructions:**
1. In a blender, combine the Isopure Protein Powder, 2% milk, and Greek yogurt.
2. Blend until smooth.
3. Add sunflower seeds for a nutty flavor and blend again.
4. Serve chilled.

### Breakfast: Oatmeal Bowl
**Ingredients:**
- 86.7 gm Oats
- 100.0 gm 2% Milk
- 30.0 gm Almonds, chopped
- 20.0 gm Honey or Maple Syrup (optional, if available)

**Instructions:**
1. In a pot, combine oats and milk. Cook over medium heat until the oats are soft and creamy.
2. Stir in chopped almonds for added crunch.
3. Sweeten with honey or maple syrup if desired.
4. Serve warm.

### Lunch: Chickpea & Tofu Salad
**Ingredients:**
- 150.0 gm Chickpeas, drained and rinsed
- 200.0 gm Firm Tofu, cubed and lightly sautéed
- 50.0 gm Nonfat Greek Yogurt (as dressing)
- Mixed greens or any vegetables (if available)

**Instructions:**
1. Sauté the cubed firm tofu in a non-stick pan until golden brown.
2. In a bowl, combine chickpeas, sautéed tofu, and any mixed greens or vegetables you have.
3. Add Greek yogurt on top as a dressing and mix well.
4. Serve chilled or at room temperature.

### Afternoon Snack: Yogurt & Pistachio Parfait
**Ingredients:**
- 70.0 gm Nonfat Greek Yogurt
- 50.0 gm Pistachios, chopped
- 50.0 gm Sunflower Seeds
- Fresh fruits like berries or banana (if available)

**Instructions:**
1. In a glass or bowl, layer Greek yogurt at the bottom.
2. Add a layer of chopped pistachios and sunflower seeds.
3. Repeat the layers until ingredients are used.
4. Top with fresh fruits if available.
5. Enjoy as a hearty snack.

### Dinner: Millet Chickpea Bowl
**Ingredients:**
- 184.7 gm Millet
- 150.0 gm Chickpeas
- 200.0 gm Firm Tofu, cubed (for protein)
- Seasoning: salt, pepper, herbs (if available)

**Instructions:**
1. Cook millet according to package instructions (usually 1 cup millet to 2 cups water; cook until tender).
2. In a skillet, sauté cubed firm tofu until golden brown.
3. Combine cooked millet, chickpeas, and sautéed tofu in a large bowl.
4. Season with salt, pepper, and any herbs or spices you prefer.
5. Serve warm.
