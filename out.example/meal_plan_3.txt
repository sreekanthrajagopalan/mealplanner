Here is a plan for a full-day menu that utilizes the specified ingredients, ensuring the total weight matches the requirements:

### Drink: Protein Smoothie
**Ingredients:**
- 50 gm Isopure Protein Powder
- 100 gm 2% Milk
- 50 gm Almonds (blended into the smoothie)

**Instructions:**
1. Blend 50 gm of Isopure Protein Powder with 100 gm of 2% milk until smooth.
2. Add 50 gm of almonds, and blend until the almonds are finely ground and incorporated.
3. Optional: Add ice for a chilled smoothie.

### Breakfast: Yogurt and Prune Parfait
**Ingredients:**
- 150 gm Nonfat Greek Yogurt
- 50 gm Prunes (chopped)
- 30 gm Millet (cooked, as a base layer)

**Instructions:**
1. Cook millet according to package instructions (you can prepare it in advance).
2. In a glass or bowl, layer the cooked millet, followed by Nonfat Greek Yogurt.
3. Top with chopped prunes for sweetness and extra fiber.

### Lunch: Rajma and Millet Bowl
**Ingredients:**
- 150 gm Rajma (cooked)
- 200 gm Millet (cooked)

**Instructions:**
1. Cook the rajma in a pressure cooker until soft (or use canned for convenience).
2. Cook the millet according to package instructions.
3. Serve the cooked rajma over a bed of millet, seasoned with salt, pepper, and any spices you like.

### Afternoon Snack: Tofu and Pistachio Energy Bites
**Ingredients:**
- 173 gm Silken Tofu
- 50 gm Pistachios (chopped)

**Instructions:**
1. Blend the silken tofu until smooth.
2. Mix in the chopped pistachios until evenly incorporated.
3. Form into small balls or bites and refrigerate for a quick snack.

### Dinner: Firm Tofu and Veggie Stir-Fry
**Ingredients:**
- 200 gm Firm Tofu (cubed)
- 200 gm assorted veggies (e.g., bell peppers, broccoli, etc.) - Optional if using any additional ingredients

**Instructions:**
1. Heat some oil in a pan and add the cubed firm tofu. Fry until golden brown.
2. Add your choice of veggies and stir-fry until tender.
3. Season with soy sauce, garlic, and ginger to taste. Serve hot.

### Summary of Ingredient Usage:
- **Isopure Protein Powder**: 50 gm (Drink)
- **2% Milk**: 100 gm (Drink)
- **Nonfat Greek Yogurt**: 150 gm (Breakfast)
- **Silken Tofu**: 173 gm (Afternoon Snack)
- **Firm Tofu**: 200 gm (Dinner)
- **Rajma**: 150 gm (Lunch)
- **Millet**: 200 gm (2 parts - Breakfast & Lunch)
- **Almonds**: 50 gm (Drink)
- **Pistachios**: 50 gm (Afternoon Snack)
- **Prunes**: 50 gm (Breakfast)

This plan fulfills the ingredient weight requirements while providing a balanced and nutritious menu for the day! Enjoy your meals!
