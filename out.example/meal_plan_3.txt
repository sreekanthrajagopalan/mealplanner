### Drink: Protein Smoothie
**Ingredients:**
- 50 g Isopure Protein Powder
- 100 g 2% Milk
- 50 g Nonfat Greek Yogurt

**Instructions:**
1. In a blender, combine the Isopure Protein Powder, 2% Milk, and Nonfat Greek Yogurt.
2. Blend until smooth and creamy.
3. Serve chilled.

### Breakfast: Greek Yogurt Parfait
**Ingredients:**
- 100 g Nonfat Greek Yogurt
- 47.7 g Fig, chopped
- 50 g Pistachios, chopped
- 25 g Oats (to top the yogurt)

**Instructions:**
1. In a bowl or glass, layer half the Greek yogurt.
2. Add the figs on top of the yogurt, then sprinkle with half of the chopped pistachios and oats.
3. Repeat the layers with the remaining Greek yogurt, figs, pistachios, and oats.
4. Serve immediately.

### Lunch: Rajma and Millet Bowl
**Ingredients:**
- 150 g Rajma, cooked
- 200 g Millet, cooked
- 100 g Firm Tofu, grilled and diced
- Spices (like cumin and chili) of your choice for flavor

**Instructions:**
1. Cook the millet according to package instructions and set aside.
2. Cook Rajma if not already prepared, and season with spices.
3. Grill the firm tofu until golden brown.
4. In a bowl, combine the millet, rajma, and grilled tofu. Mix well.
5. Serve warm.

### Afternoon Snack: Oatmeal with Walnuts
**Ingredients:**
- 71.4 g Oats
- 50 g Walnuts, chopped
- 100 g 2% Milk (to cook the oats)

**Instructions:**
1. In a saucepan, bring the milk to a boil.
2. Stir in the oats and reduce the heat. Cook until the oats are soft.
3. Remove from heat and stir in the chopped walnuts.
4. Allow to cool slightly before serving.

### Dinner: Tofu and Rajma Stir-fry with Millet
**Ingredients:**
- 100 g Firm Tofu, diced
- 150 g Rajma, cooked
- 200 g Millet (you can use the leftover from lunch)
- Spices (like turmeric, salt, and pepper) to taste

**Instructions:**
1. Heat a non-stick pan over medium heat and saut√© the diced tofu until golden brown.
2. Add the cooked rajma and your choice of seasonings; stir well.
3. Cook until everything is heated through.
4. Serve over cooked millet.
