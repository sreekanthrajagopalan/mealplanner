Here's a meal plan using the provided ingredients, respecting the total weight and including a drink, breakfast, lunch, afternoon snack, and dinner.

### Drink: Protein Smoothie
**Ingredients:**
- 25 g Isopure Protein Powder
- 100 g 2% Milk
- 75 g Nonfat Greek Yogurt

**Instructions:**
1. In a blender, combine the Isopure Protein Powder, 2% milk, and the Greek yogurt.
2. Blend until smooth.
3. Pour into a glass and enjoy.

### Breakfast: Greek Yogurt Parfait
**Ingredients:**
- 75 g Nonfat Greek Yogurt
- 50 g Oats
- 20 g Pistachios (chopped)
- 20 g Fig (chopped)

**Instructions:**
1. In a bowl, layer the Greek yogurt at the bottom.
2. Add oats on top.
3. Sprinkle chopped pistachios and figs over the oats.
4. Serve immediately.

### Lunch: Rajma & Millet Bowl
**Ingredients:**
- 100 g Rajma (cooked)
- 150 g Millet (cooked)
- 100 g Firm Tofu (cubed and sautéed)

**Instructions:**
1. Cook the millet according to package instructions. Set aside.
2. Cook the rajma if not pre-cooked and season to taste.
3. In a non-stick pan, sauté the cubed firm tofu until golden brown.
4. In a bowl, combine cooked rajma, millet, and sautéed tofu. Mix well and serve warm.

### Afternoon Snack: Nutty Oatmeal
**Ingredients:**
- 50 g Oats
- 20 g Walnuts (chopped)
- 200 g 2% Milk

**Instructions:**
1. In a saucepan, combine oats and 2% milk. Cook until the oatmeal is thick and creamy.
2. Once cooked, stir in the chopped walnuts.
3. Serve warm, optionally topped with a splash of honey or fresh fruit if available.

### Dinner: Tofu and Rajma Stir-fry
**Ingredients:**
- 100 g Firm Tofu (cubed)
- 50 g Rajma (cooked)
- 100 g Vegetables of choice (e.g., bell peppers, broccoli; optional)
- Spices (cumin, coriander, salt, and pepper to taste)

**Instructions:**
1. In a pan, heat a little oil and add the cubed tofu. Stir-fry until golden.
2. Add cooked rajma and vegetables, stirring to combine.
3. Season with spices to taste and cook for an additional 5-7 minutes until heated through.
4. Serve warm.
