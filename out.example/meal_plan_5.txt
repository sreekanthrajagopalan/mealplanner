### Drink: Protein Smoothie
**Ingredients:**
- Isopure Protein Powder: 25 gm
- 2% Milk: 100 gm
- Nonfat Greek Yogurt: 50 gm
- Almonds: 10 gm (for garnish)

**Instructions:**
1. In a blender, combine 25 gm of Isopure protein powder, 100 gm of 2% milk, and 50 gm of nonfat Greek yogurt.
2. Blend until smooth.
3. Serve in a glass and sprinkle 10 gm of chopped almonds on top for added crunch.

**Total Weight:** 25 + 100 + 50 + 10 = 185 gm

---

### Breakfast: Yogurt Parfait
**Ingredients:**
- Nonfat Greek Yogurt: 100 gm
- Oats: 50 gm
- Almonds: 10 gm
- Pistachios: 10 gm

**Instructions:**
1. In a bowl, layer 100 gm of Greek yogurt and sprinkle 50 gm of oats.
2. Top with 10 gm of chopped almonds and 10 gm of chopped pistachios for crunch.
3. Serve chilled.

**Total Weight:** 100 + 50 + 10 + 10 = 170 gm

---

### Lunch: Tofu Rajma Bowl
**Ingredients:**
- Firm Tofu: 200 gm (cubed)
- Rajma: 150 gm (cooked)
- Millet: 200 gm (cooked)

**Instructions:**
1. Cook the millet according to package instructions.
2. In a pan, sauté the cubed tofu until golden brown.
3. Add the cooked rajma to the pan and mix until heated through.
4. Serve the tofu and rajma mixture over a bed of millet.

**Total Weight:** 200 + 150 + 200 = 550 gm

---

### Afternoon Snack: Oat and Nut Energy Bites
**Ingredients:**
- Oats: 50 gm
- Almonds: 10 gm
- Pecans: 34.9 gm
- 2% Milk: 20 gm (to help bind the mixture)

**Instructions:**
1. In a bowl, mix 50 gm of oats, 10 gm of chopped almonds, and 34.9 gm of chopped pecans.
2. Gradually add 20 gm of milk into the mixture until it holds together.
3. Roll into small balls and refrigerate for at least 30 minutes before serving.

**Total Weight:** 50 + 10 + 34.9 + 20 = 114.9 gm

---

### Dinner: Millet Tofu Stir-Fry
**Ingredients:**
- Firm Tofu: 200 gm (diced)
- Millet: 100 gm (cooked, from previous meal)
- Rajma: 100 gm (remaining)

**Instructions:**
1. In a pan, stir-fry the diced tofu until golden and crisp.
2. Add in 100 gm of cooked rajma and mix well.
3. Serve over the cooked millet.

**Total Weight:** 200 + 100 + 100 = 400 gm
