### Drink: Protein Smoothie
**Ingredients:**
- 50g Isopure Protein Powder
- 100g 2% Milk
- 100g Nonfat Greek Yogurt

**Instructions:**
1. In a blender, combine the Isopure Protein Powder, 2% Milk, and Nonfat Greek Yogurt.
2. Blend until smooth and creamy. You can add ice for a chilled smoothie if desired.
3. Pour into a glass and enjoy!

### Breakfast: Millet Porridge with Almonds and Pecans
**Ingredients:**
- 200g Millet
- 100g Oats
- 20g Almonds (chopped)
- 34.9g Pecans (chopped)

**Instructions:**
1. Rinse millet and oats under cold water.
2. In a pot, combine the millet and oats with 3 cups of water. Bring to a boil, then reduce heat and simmer until the grains are tender (about 20 minutes).
3. Stir in the chopped almonds and pecans.
4. Serve warm, adding honey or fruit on top if desired.

### Lunch: Rajma Salad Bowl
**Ingredients:**
- 150g Rajma (cooked)
- 200g Firm Tofu (cubed)
- Mixed greens (optional for base)
- Any dressing of choice (to taste)

**Instructions:**
1. If using raw tofu, press it to remove excess moisture, and then sauté it in a pan until golden brown.
2. In a bowl, layer your mixed greens (if using), then add the cooked rajma on top.
3. Add the sautéed tofu.
4. Drizzle with your choice of dressing and toss gently.

### Afternoon Snack: Greek Yogurt with Pistachios
**Ingredients:**
- 150g Nonfat Greek Yogurt
- 50g Pistachios (shelled)

**Instructions:**
1. In a bowl, add 150g of Nonfat Greek Yogurt.
2. Top with shelled pistachios.
3. You can add honey or fruit for extra flavor, if desired.
4. Enjoy this protein-packed snack!

### Dinner: Tofu and Rajma Millet Bowl
**Ingredients:**
- 200g Firm Tofu (cubed)
- 150g Rajma (cooked)
- 200g Millet (cooked)
- Seasonings of choice (like cumin, turmeric, salt, and pepper)

**Instructions:**
1. Sauté the tofu in a little bit of oil until golden brown.
2. Add the cooked rajma to the pan with the tofu and season with cumin, turmeric, salt, and pepper. Cook until heated through.
3. In a bowl, serve a base of cooked millet, then top with the tofu and rajma mixture.
4. Enjoy your nutritious dinner!
